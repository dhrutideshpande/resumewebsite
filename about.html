My name is Dhruti

I’m a recent MBA grad from Yale School of Management (SOM). I’m interested in working in marketing and innovation focusing on how consumer insights can inform marketing strategy and product design.

At SOM, I completed a research project with Viacom Labs and the Yale Center for Customer Insights to understand the limits of brand permissibility and how engaging fans through other more highly engaged fans can allow Viacom to connect with their consumers in new ways.

Between my first and second year of SOM, I interned Jose Andres’ restaurant group, ThinkFoodGroup, developing products with the R&D team and creating a social media campaign with the marketing team for 25 Jose Andres concepts.

Prior to SOM, I spent five years consulting for Accenture, focusing on customer experience, process design, and project management. I worked on technology, strategy, and management projects across a variety of industries and functions.
